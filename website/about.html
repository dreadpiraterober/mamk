Midland City Market

Hello. I'm dread_pirate_rober. This is an anonymous marketplace called Midland City Market. You can buy and sell items here.

Peer-to-Peer Marketplace

Midland City Market is a peer-to-peer (P2P) marketplace where buyers and sellers interact directly with each other. The platform acts only as a marketplace and is not a party to transactions between users.

Users are responsible for their own communications, transactions, agreements, and decisions. Always verify information carefully and protect your privacy when interacting with other users.

Security Note

Always protect your account with a strong password and do not share it with anyone.

⚠️ Forgot Password Warning:
If you forget your password, we do not have permission or ability to recover your session.
Please use an external secure storage (such as an encrypted USB drive or a password manager) to safely store your credentials.

Platform Rules

⚠️ If any user attempts to spam, abuse, disrupt, or misuse the platform, their access may be restricted or permanently removed. Their listings and content may also be removed in order to maintain a fair and functional marketplace for all users.

Please treat other users with respect and help keep the platform operational. Consider Midland City Market as a shared community marketplace. Damaging the platform ultimately harms the entire community, including yourself.

Privacy

This system uses hashed passwords and session-based authentication for basic account security.

Users should avoid sharing unnecessary personal information and should take reasonable steps to protect their own privacy online.
